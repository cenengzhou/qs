See what's new in QS 2.0

Once you have experienced QS 2.0, you will know how
easy a month end process can be.

  • Extractable Data

    Everything is extractable. Whenever you see the data that is displayed in a grid, you can always download a copy for record keeping or data analysis.

  • Reports

    Reports can be generated in PDF and Excel format at anytime you want with just a click. No more queuing. No more waiting.

  • Integrated with JDE

    Not sure about what you have posted to Finance? Our Enquiry functions help you quickly and effortlessly find what you want.

  • Easy to use

    Navigation bar on the top, sub-menu on the left plus an expandable sidebar on the right to help you out anytime. That's it! See, you already know how to use it.
